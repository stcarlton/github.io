2022

Description

2022 is a complete 3D, arcade style top down shooter. Shoot zombies, level up and gain new abilities and talents, increasing your power to maintain pace with infinitely scaling enemies. Hunt down and kill elites to gain items that grant powerful abilities and makes each play through unique. What level can you reach?

Technical Overview
